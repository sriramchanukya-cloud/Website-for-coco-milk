The ACV Chronicles

Our Legacy

In 2026, the trio launched ACV—a brand name cleverly derived from the first initials of Asa Latha, Chanukya, and Vijay. The startup transforms discarded coconut ingredients into high-protein, zero-waste coconut milk products. By uniting their strengths, they solved a major environmental problem: the massive waste generated daily by local coconut vendors. Today, ACV is a staple plant-based dairy alternative in households nationwide.

The Breakthrough

The idea spark came during a family trip to a coastal region, where Chanukya noticed vendors dumping tons of coconut shells and leftover water daily. Chanukya, a food technology graduate, realized this "waste" still held incredible nutritional value. Back home, Asa Latha used her traditional culinary expertise to formulate an ultra-creamy coconut milk recipe fortified with natural plant proteins. Vijay then stepped in, designing a custom, low-cost extraction machine in their backyard to process the ingredients efficiently.
